    ABOUT US

    As one of the leading companies in the Energy Sector, BURSA BAKIR KABLO LTD. ŞTİ.
    took its first step in the sector in 2007 with the sale of copper products and electrical materials, and continues to maintain its position in the sector with the Manufacturing of Grounding Materials, Cable and Transport Systems.
    Our main field of activity includes Public and Private Sector Buildings, Industrial Facilities, Factories and similar projects.
    Our sales and marketing team, backed by the support of our specialized production staff, establishes direct communication with companies in 62 provinces of Turkey, providing solutions that meet our customers' needs while following up on business processes and supporting projects.

    • Our Mission as BURSA BAKIR:
      To provide our high-quality manufactured materials to customers in the fastest and most economical way while ensuring continuity.
    • Our Vision as BURSA BAKIR:
      To have production capacity with high quality and technology, and to be among the preferred companies both domestically and internationally.
    • Our Values:
      To prioritize people, maintain honesty and transparency, ethical business discipline, and to be innovative, dynamic, and sensitive to the environment and society while continuously improving our work.
    • Our Quality Policy:
      To ensure customer satisfaction in all areas of our operations and effectively manage our resources to meet our customers' expectations, to support the development and participation of our employees for continuous improvement of our service quality, to contribute to the national economy with respect to our company's values, environment and society.
      Our determination to work, discipline, what we have done and what we will do are our greatest examples.
